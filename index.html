Features

How It Works

Three steps. Zero effort.

Install & Allow

Grant location and motion permissions. Drivio needs these to detect driving automatically.

Just Drive

Put your phone in your pocket. Drivio detects vehicle motion, starts recording, and stops when you park.

Review & Export

Confirm trips, tag categories, export mileage reports. Everything is organized and ready for you.
