Features

Built for every kind of drive

Your commute, your errands, your road trips — Drivio records them all. What you do with them is up to you.

🚗

Auto Trip Detection

No start or stop buttons. Drivio detects driving from GPS, motion, and Bluetooth/CarPlay signals — then records the route hands-free.

📊

Daily Trip Insights

Your daily commute, weekend errands, and road trips — all auto-logged. A complete trip history so you can see where you go, when you go, and how often.

📍

Build Your Journey

Pick a few memorable drives — your weekend to Big Sur, your road trip to the coast. Group them, drop memory pins along the route, and photos from your library auto-match by time and place.

📄

Shareable Posters

Turn any journey into a stunning vertical poster — route map, stats, and photos — ready to post on Instagram or send to family.

📡

Live Share

Share your drive with family in real time. Like Find My live location, but only while you're actually on the road — and only with the people you choose.

🔒

Private by Design

A privacy-first trip app. Everything stays on your device — Drivio has no backend server, so we never see your data. No analytics, no ad trackers, no third-party services. Just Apple's own.

How It Works

Drive. Curate. Share.

Drive

Just go. Drivio quietly records every drive in the background — no apps to open, no timers to start.

Curate

Pick the drives that mattered — a road trip, a scenic detour, a memorable weekend. Group them into a Journey with photos and memory pins.

Share

Export your journey as a beautiful poster. Post it, print it, or keep it as your own private keepsake.
